Courses at Hogwarts

Astronomy

Look up to the stars and discover the mysteries of the universe in this celestial course. Learn about the movements of the planets and constellations, and perhaps even glimpse a shooting star.

Charms

Bring everyday objects to life with a flick of your wand in this enchanting course. From unlocking doors to conjuring up a patronus, you'll learn to master the art of charms and incantations.

Defense Against The Dark Arts

Protect yourself from the forces of evil in this vital course. From battling boggarts to defending against the unforgivable curses, you'll learn the spells and strategies needed to defend against the darkest magic.

Herbology

Discover the magical properties of plants and fungi in this fascinating course. From brewing potions to tending to magical plants, you'll learn the secrets of the wizarding world's green thumb.

Potions

Mix up a little magic with this course in potion-making. From curing common ailments to crafting powerful elixirs, you'll learn the art of potion-making and the subtle art of ingredient selection.

Transfiguration

Transform objects and creatures with the wave of your wand in this transformative course. From turning a teapot into a tortoise to transfiguring yourself into a cat, you'll learn the intricacies of transfiguration and the power of imagination.

Care of Magical Creatures

Care for and learn about the magical creatures that inhabit the wizarding world. From hippogriffs to unicorns, you'll gain an understanding of magical creatures and how to properly care for them.

Electives

Discover a world of magical hobbies and interests in our extracurricular activities. From the art of divination to the strategy of wizard's chess, you'll find something to spark your interest and expand your magical horizons.
